The '''French Martini''' was invented in the 1980s at one of [[Keith McNally]]'s [[New York City]] bars. It next appeared on the drinks menu at McNally's [[Balthazar (restaurant)|Balthazar]] in [[SoHo, Manhattan|SoHo]] in 1996. The cocktail was produced during the 1980s-1990s cocktail Renaissance.
The key ingredient that makes a Martini "French" is [[Chambord (liqueur)|Chambord]], a black raspberry [[liqueur]] that has been produced in [[France]] since 1685.<ref>[http://iba-world.com/new-era-drinks/french-martini/  French Martini] iba-world.com</ref>
