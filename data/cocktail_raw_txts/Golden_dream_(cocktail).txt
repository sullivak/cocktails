The '''Golden Dream''' is an [[IBA official cocktail]] made with [[Galliano (drink)|Galliano]] and [[Cointreau]].  It is classed as an "after dinner" drink.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.iba-world.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=88 | title=IBA Official Cocktails | publisher=International Bartenders Association | accessdate=8 February 2015}}</ref>

The Golden Dream was popular during the 60s and 70s and originated at the Old King Bar in Miami, mixed by Raimundo Alvarez.<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Maynard|first1=Nora|title=Straight Up: Golden Dream Cocktail for Oscar Night|journal=Kitchn|date=22 February 2008|url=https://www.thekitchn.com/straight-up-golden-dream-cockt-43430|accessdate=29 November 2017}}</ref>

The cocktail was dedicated to actress Joan Crawford and became quite popular at the end of the 1960s on the east coast of the [[United States]].
