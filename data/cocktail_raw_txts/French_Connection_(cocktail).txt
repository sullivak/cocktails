A '''French Connection''' is a [[List of cocktails|cocktail]] made with equal parts [[Cognac (drink)|Cognac]] and [[Amaretto]] liqueur.<ref name="iba">{{cite web|url=http://iba-world.com/cocktails/french-connection |accessdate=15 February 2017 |title=IBA Official Cocktails |deadurl=no |df= }}</ref> The cocktail is named for the [[Gene Hackman]] film of the [[The French Connection (film)|same name]].<ref>{{Cite book| publisher = Skyhorse Publishing, Inc.| isbn = 978-1-62873-175-0| last = Hallay| first = Amanda| title = Vintage Cocktails: Retro Recipes for the Home Mixologist| date = 2011-10-10 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DGAtAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA77}}</ref>
