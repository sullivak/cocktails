The '''Angel Face''' is a [[cocktail]] made from [[gin]], [[apricot brandy]] and [[Calvados]] in equal amounts.<ref>Harry Craddock - The Savoy Cocktail Book 1930</ref>

The cocktail first appears in the [[Savoy_Hotel#The_Savoy_Cocktail_Book|Savoy Cocktail Book]] compiled by [[Harry Craddock]] in 1930.<ref>https://www.diffordsguide.com/en-au/cocktails/recipe/521/angel-face</ref><ref>http://intoxicologist.net/2014/12/angel-face-classic-cocktail/</ref><ref>http://intoxicologist.net/2014/12/angel-face-classic-cocktail/</ref>
